More of NetPal

Marketplace designed and exclusive to university/college students.The marketplace will display products, tasks, and events; all of which are targeted to students. This increases security, due to encouraging students to trade in campus. There is also an existing validating system such as student numbers that can easily verify users, increasing trust. Revenue can be acquired through obtaining account fees, % of an exchange, ads hosted by businesses.

What is NetPal?

NetPal is an app created by students with in mind to bring something to help the students face the university struggles. Being in school isn't always easy this is why we created NetPal! It is easy to use, takes seconds to register and will help you accomplish daily tasks and make money! Sounds like magic, but it’s simple: you just register and you can post services that you are able to provide for a good price. You can in this way make money and also benefit from others that offers a variety of services: Dog walking, tutoring, ride shares, meal prep and more!
